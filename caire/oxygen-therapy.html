HOW IT WORKS

CAIRE's product line of oxygen concentrators concentrates the ambient air around us in order to guarantee you are never left without the oxygen you need.

Who Needs It

Doctors prescribe oxygen therapy to individuals who are not maintaining sufficient oxygen levels for their body to properly function.

Stay Active

With oxygen therapy, patients with respiratory needs can return to the little things that mean the most. From cooking dinner to cheering on your grandson's little league team, breathe easier with CAIRE.

Benefits

With oxygen therapy, your doctor prescribes the level of oxygen you need to be your best. Patients often experience more energy, relief from stress, and greater relaxation.
